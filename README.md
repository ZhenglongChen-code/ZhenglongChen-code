## 👨‍🎓 About Me

I am a Master’s student at the **Research Center for Mathematics and Interdisciplinary Sciences, Shandong University**.
My research lies at the intersection of **computational mathematics** and **machine learning**, with a focus on reliable and efficient numerical methods for scientific computing.

I am particularly interested in:
- Numerical methods for PDEs
- Uncertainty quantification
- Physics-informed deep learning
- Data-driven reservoir simulation

---

- **Computational Mathematics**
